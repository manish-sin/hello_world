About

BetteRoads is an open-source project trying to address the issue of poor roads by bringing comprehensive data of road conditions to all through a website. This would empower people to appropriately raise concerns of poor roads with the authorities backed with real data. For example, a person can compare road conditions near him, with more privileged areas and can raise such issue at least during Election. This is in the hope that we get the serious attention of authorities on our road conditions, when authorities are serious we can expect them t do timely maintenance, which in turn should save taxpayers money due to untimely maintenance. How this works?

Any person can record a video of roads from his phone using a dedicated app. Videos can be recorded while commuting to your office or going shopping. The recorded video will be uploaded to the BetteRoads cloud. Developers of BetterRoads are developing a software-based on Machine learning which will process the video to identify potholes on the roads. Then the software will plot a map showing the locations of the identified potholes. This map will be available for all on the betteroads.net website.
